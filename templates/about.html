Our Mission

DiabetesCare is an advanced machine learning-based diabetes prediction and prevention platform designed to empower individuals with information about their diabetes risk. Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible and help people take proactive steps toward better health.

We believe that early detection and prevention are key to managing diabetes effectively. By combining cutting-edge machine learning technology with evidence-based medical guidelines, DiabetesCare provides personalized insights and actionable recommendations to help you maintain optimal health.

How It Works

Our Prediction Algorithm

DiabetesCare uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset. The model analyzes 8 key health metrics:

  • Pregnancies: Number of pregnancies (for women)
  • Glucose: Fasting glucose level (mg/dL)
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure (mmHg)
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
  • Insulin: 2-hour serum insulin (mu U/ml)
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (kg/m²)
  • DPF: Diabetes Pedigree Function
  • Age: Patient age (years)

Accuracy & Validation

Our machine learning model achieves approximately 78-82% accuracy on the test dataset. However, please note that this tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice from qualified healthcare providers.
