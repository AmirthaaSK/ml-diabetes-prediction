Patient Health Information

Enter your health metrics for accurate diabetes risk assessment

Important: Please provide accurate health measurements for the most reliable prediction results. All fields are required for comprehensive assessment.

Health Metrics Guidelines

  • Glucose: Fasting blood glucose level in mg/dL. Typical range: 70-100 mg/dL for non-fasting individuals
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure reading in mmHg. Normal range: 60-80 mmHg
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skin fold thickness measurement in millimeters
  • Insulin: 2-hour serum insulin level in milli-international units per milliliter
  • BMI: Body Mass Index calculated as weight (kg) divided by height squared (m²)
  • Age: Patient age in complete years. Minimum age requirement: 18 years
  • Diabetes Pedigree Factor: Family history of diabetes represented as a numeric value
  • Disclaimer: This prediction is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical consultation or diagnosis
